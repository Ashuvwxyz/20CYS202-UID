What is ANOKHA?

Anokha Techfest, the annual event of Amrita School of Engineering, Coimbatore, provides students with a unique oppurtunity for students to participate in fun-filled and challenging events and workshops. The event throws open oppurtunities for students from all disciplines to challenge themselves and foster the "Can do" attitude.

Things to expect

Not only is Anokha a techfest, but also a stage for all participants to showcase their talents and interests. Across this 3 day fest, several events will be hosted by the different student disciplines present, so that there is something for everyone!

Here are a few key events that are to be held:

  • CSE - Competitive programming contest
  • Aero - CanSat event
  • EEE & Mech - RoboSoccer
  • CYS - Xiomara : Capture the Flag
  • Mathematics - Matholympics

There are also other events just for enjoyment!

  • Lumiere - Workshops and talks
  • Armageddon - Gaming
  • Eventide - Music and much more

Event FAQs

Who all can register?

Anyone with a valid college ID can register! It is a college event after all :)

What all are included in the pass?

Once you get the pass, you can use it to get 3 day accomodation and food inside the campus.

I forgot my login credentials. What do I do?

No worries. You can use the forget password feature provided in the login page.

I bought a pass but will not be able to attend. Will I be refunded?

Sorry, but there are no refunds for purchased passes.
